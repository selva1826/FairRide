The Problem We're Solving

Current Ride-Hailing Challenges

🚫 For Riders:

  • • Unexpected Price Hikes: Surge pricing without transparency
  • • Driver Cancellations: 40% of bookings get cancelled after acceptance
  • • Hidden Charges: Extra demands for cash payments
  • • Poor Service: No accountability for driver behavior

🚫 For Drivers:

  • • Unfair Fixed Pricing: Can't negotiate based on traffic/distance
  • • Platform Control: No say in fare determination
  • • Low Earnings: Fixed rates don't account for fuel costs
  • • Forced Acceptance: Penalty for rejecting low-profit rides

Our Revolutionary Solution

Competitive Bidding System

🎯 How It Works:

  1. Rider Books: Sets pickup/destination, ride goes live
  2. Drivers Compete: 2-minute bidding window with custom pricing
  3. Best Price Wins: Lowest bidder gets the ride automatically
  4. Transparent Process: Real-time updates for both parties
  5. Fair Outcome: Market-driven pricing benefits everyone

🏆 Key Innovation:

  • ✓ Custom Price Input: Drivers type exact amounts
  • ✓ Distance-Based Bidding: Smart suggestions based on route
  • ✓ Live Competition: Real-time bid tracking
  • ✓ Automatic Assignment: Fastest fair system
